Nocturne - Charles Henze (1872-1946)

This song is very sweet and very expressive. I first heard this piece played
by Iwao Suzuki from his album Guitar for beginner "Classical Series".
If you have not ever heard this piece you must get it! 

If there are any problems or corrections email: yose_lasibey@yahoo.com

This Nocturne is one of my favorite pieces to play and I would like to dedicate
this song to Yuni Kurniyatiningsih, Kandangan Temanggung Jateng Indonesia,
"Hai......... "

Legend:
======

Dynamics:
      MF (Mezzo Forte)        : Medium Loud
      F  (Forte)              : Loud or Strong
      P  (Piano)              : Soft
      < Cres (Cresendo)       : Gradualy Louder
      > Decres (Decresendo)   : Gradualy Softer
      PP (Piano Pizzimo)      : More Soft
      MP (Mezzo Piano)        : Medium Soft
Signs:
      D.C (Da Capo)   : Go back to the beginning and repeat
      CODA            : Final Section or Passage
      *||             : Repeat (go back to the beginning and
                        continue to the next bar
Tempo:
      Andantino         : A little faster than Andante
      Andante           : "Going"
      Rit (Ritardando)  : Retard or Slow down
      Rall (Ralentando) : More Slow
      Morendo           : Softly
