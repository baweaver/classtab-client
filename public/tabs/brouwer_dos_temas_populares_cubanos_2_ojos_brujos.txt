Dos Temas Populares Cubanos - 2. Ojos Brujos (Les Yeux Sorciers)
(Sur un théme de Roig) - by Leo Brouwer

This is the second piece of the volume DOS TEMAS POPULARES CUBANOS called 
OJOS BRUJOS. This piece needs to be played moderately and clearly. The 
melody from this masterpiece is very interesting though it is a little 
difficult to understand, which is a difference between OJOS BRUJOS and the 
first piece of the volume called CANCIÓN DE CUNA (Sur un théme de Grenet) 
whose melody is very clear.  If you have any questions or corrections ask 
Weed (weed@wussu.com) or mail me.

Tabbed by Salvador Mauricio Delgado Guerra – 13/06/03
rey_diamante@hotmail.com
