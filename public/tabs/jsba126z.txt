Musette (from the Anna Magdelena Notebook)  - J.S.Bach
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Arranged for guitar by John Kean (keanj@agresearch.cri.nz).

This is an easy classical piece that is fun to play.
It was originally written by Bach to help teach the keyboard
to one of his many wives (Anna Magdelena).

******************************************************
Anh 126 - Musette in D
AFAIK Anna Magdelena was his 2nd (and last) wife
weed@wussu.com - 26 April 00
******************************************************

A "musette" is a piece with a droning bass-line, like a bagpipe.

Drop-D tuning. (DADGBE)

2/4 time.
