J.S. Bach, Sarabande from Cello Suite #5, BWV 1011

Arranged for guitar by Chris Mooney - CAhBach@aol.com

This is the only movement from any of the cello suites that
is not polyphonic. 'Slava calls this movement "Darkness" and
the greatest example of Bach's musical genius.

Standard tuning

3/4 time, very even and deliberate tempo
