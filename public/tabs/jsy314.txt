Tabbed by The_S (March 2003) - hermanss@iprimus.com.au

The_S: This is a piece whos name i hadn't found for quite sometime.
       It was used on the Simpsons when Homer eats the chips in space.
       And it has been used many a time in movies where actors are
       dancing a waltz I have yet to record myself playing each guitar
       and combine the two recordings to hear what this tab sounds like.
       Never-the-less, enjoy this wonderful piece.
