                 Ferdenando Carulli
                     Valce in C
             Transcribed by Tarek Yazbek

 This is a very lovly and easy piece by  F. Carulli.
 It is played in the key of C then it changes to the
 key of Am. For any questions or corrections, please
 email me on  yazbek999@hotmail.com
 Its time signature is 3\8.  The tuning is standard.
